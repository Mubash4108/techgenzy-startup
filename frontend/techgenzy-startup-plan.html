🎯 Vision & Mission

Our Vision:

To become the leading platform that transforms beginners into professional web developers and entrepreneurs, creating a community where young innovators can learn, build, and launch their tech startups.

Our Mission:

Provide accessible, project-based web development education combined with entrepreneurship guidance, helping Gen Z build both technical skills and business acumen to create meaningful tech solutions.

🎪 Target Audience:

  • High school/college students interested in tech
  • Career changers wanting to enter web development
  • Young entrepreneurs with ideas but no coding skills
  • Self-taught developers looking for structured learning

💰 Business Model

Revenue Streams:

  • Freemium Model: Basic courses free, premium content ($9.99/month)
  • Project Templates: Sell customizable website templates ($29-$99)
  • Mentorship: 1-on-1 coaching sessions ($50/hour)
  • Certification: Industry-recognized certificates ($49)
  • Job Placement: Premium job matching service (10% of first year salary)
  • Corporate Training: B2B courses for companies

🎯 First Year Goals:

  • 10,000 free users
  • 1,000 paying subscribers
  • $50,000 monthly recurring revenue
  • 50 successful student placements
