🚀 Deploy TechGenzY to Netlify (100% FREE)

✅ Netlify Hosting = FREE FOREVER
❌ Custom Domain = PAID (optional)
You can use FREE subdomain like: techgenzy-awesome.netlify.app

Step 1: Go to Netlify

Open netlify.com in your browser

Step 2: Sign Up (Free)

Click "Sign up" → Choose "Continue with Google" or "Continue with GitHub"

This is FREE and takes 30 seconds

Step 3: Deploy Your Site

After signing up, you'll see the dashboard.

Look for "Sites" → Click "Deploy manually" or "Add new site"

Step 4: Upload Your File

Simply drag and drop your techgenzy-landing-page.html file

Or create a folder with all your HTML files and drag the folder

Step 5: Get Your FREE URL

Netlify will give you a URL like:

https://amazing-site-123.netlify.app

You can change "amazing-site-123" to something like "techgenzy-landing"

🎉 CONGRATULATIONS!

Your TechGenzY website is now live on the internet!

Share this URL with friends, family, and potential users.

Step 6: Update Your Site (Optional)

To update your site:

  • Go to your Netlify dashboard
  • Click on your site
  • Click "Deploys"
  • Drag & drop the updated file
